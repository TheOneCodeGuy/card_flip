Speed Test

In case you suspect that the network is not working properly in your area, please use the speed test at this link: LAN Speed Test. Your IP address and speed will be automatically recorded. Any feedback that could help us debug problems is greatly appreciated. Note on the speed test: this only tells you whether your connection to the CC core is good and helps us debug switch level problems.
